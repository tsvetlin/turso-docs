## Getting Started

If you're new to Turso, the easiest way to get started is by following the [Quickstart](/quickstart).

## Discord

Join the community and the Turso team on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/4B5D7hYwub). You can show off what you're working on, ask for help, and suggest what we should work on next.

## Security

If you spot something that doesn't look right, or you've discovered a security related bug or issue, reach out to us via email [security@turso.tech](mailto:security@turso.tech).

## FAQs

If you have a question about your account or billing, you'll find answers to most [frequently asked questions](https://turso.tech/pricing) on our website.
